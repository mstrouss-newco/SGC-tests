Questions

Everything you want to know.

Who qualifies?
Applicants must be at least 18 years old, reside in the U.S., be a mother or primary caregiver, and currently be navigating financial hardship. Qualifying does not guarantee selection, but every applicant is reviewed with care.
How long does the application take?
About two minutes. You'll provide your name, date of birth, and phone number, and confirm with an SMS code. Standard Giving then verifies your situation with a soft credit check. If selected, you'll be notified by email.
Will this affect my credit score?
No. A soft credit pull is used strictly to verify your financial situation and does not affect your credit score. It is not visible to lenders or other parties.
What financial information is visible?
Standard Giving sees a soft credit report: credit card balances, outstanding loans, delinquencies, and your overall financial snapshot. This is used only to confirm the hardship you describe, and is kept confidential.
How are recipients selected?
Standard Giving collects and verifies applicant information through the secure application process. Final grant decisions are made by Global Empowerment Mission Inc., the 501(c)(3) public charity that reviews each applicant and distributes all funds. Every application is read with care — qualifying does not guarantee selection, but we do our best to reach the women where support will make the biggest difference.
Who is behind this campaign?
This is a Paris Hilton Mother's Day campaign in partnership with Standard Giving Co., which verifies applicants and operates the platform. Funds are distributed by Global Empowerment Mission Inc., a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN: 45-3782061) with a 4-star Charity Navigator rating.
